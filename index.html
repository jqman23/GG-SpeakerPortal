Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the 2026 Global Gathering?

A Global Gathering for the Future of Child Welfare is a virtual convening taking place October 6–8, 2026. Day 1 features Skill Building Institutes. Days 2 and 3 include keynotes, workshops, strategy sessions, international exchanges, creative spaces, and connection opportunities.

What should I do first?

Complete the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. This helps us confirm your session details, CEU needs, recording status, video format, and any pre-recording plans.

How do I check my session details?

Use the Session Information Lookup tab to search by speaker name or session title. If something looks wrong, contact globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

What do CEU-eligible presenters need to submit?

Please submit 3 measurable objectives and 3 knowledge-check questions through the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. A draft helper is available inside the questionnaire.

Will my session be recorded?

Recording status varies by session. Select your session in the Speaker Questionnaire or use the Session Information Lookup tab to review what we currently have on file.

What is the difference between Zoom and Embedded?

Zoom sessions use a standard Zoom-based setup connected to the virtual event experience. Embedded sessions are built directly into Attendee Hub. Your questionnaire will show the format currently listed for your session.

What are Skill Building Institutes?

Skill Building Institutes are extended, hands-on learning sessions on Day 1. They are designed for deeper practice, applied tools, and skill development.

When will I get Attendee Hub access?

Presenters will receive Attendee Hub instructions by email before the event. Please use the email connected to your speaker profile.

How do I host or start my session?

Once Attendee Hub is ready, we will share host-access instructions. If you are assigned as a host, you will access your session through the session page in Attendee Hub.

Who do I contact for help?

Email the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

Attendee Hub

Attendee Hub will be the virtual event platform for the 2026 Global Gathering. Presenters and attendees will use it to access the event experience, including session pages, live or embedded session access, event materials, and engagement features that may include chat, Q&A, polling, and on-demand content.

Presenter guidance will be added here as the Attendee Hub setup is finalized. This will include instructions for accessing your session, understanding your session format, preparing materials, and knowing what to expect before the event opens.
