Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I know if my session is CEU Eligible?

Use the Session Information Lookup tab above and follow the prompts. CEU eligibility will also be reflected in Attendee Hub once the program and session details are finalized.

What is the 2026 Global Gathering, and when does it take place?

A Global Gathering for the Future of Child Welfare is a three-day virtual convening taking place October 6–8, 2026. Day 1 features Skill Building Institutes, and Days 2 and 3 feature the Global Gathering program, including keynotes, workshops, solution-oriented strategy sessions, international exchanges, creative spaces, and opportunities for connection. This Speaker Support & Resource Page is designed to help presenters prepare for their role in that virtual experience.

What are Skill Building Institutes?

Skill Building Institutes are immersive, hands-on learning experiences offered on Day 1 of the Gathering. These extended sessions are designed for practical skill development, deeper learning, and applied frameworks that support future-focused child welfare practice and leadership. If you are presenting or supporting a Skill Building Institute, please pay close attention to any additional instructions from the Global Gathering Team, since these sessions may require more preparation than shorter sessions.

Do presenters need to register for the Gathering?

Yes. All presenters must be registered for the 2026 Global Gathering in order to access Attendee Hub and host or participate in their session(s). If you are unsure whether you are registered, use the Session Information Lookup tab on this page. If your registration status does not look correct, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

What do CEU-eligible presenters need to submit?

For CEU-eligible sessions, at least one presenter should submit three knowledge-check questions and three measurable learning objectives through the Speaker Portal & Questionnaire by the deadline communicated by the Global Gathering Team. Questions should be based on the content of the session itself and should not be about the Global Gathering, the session format, or presenter biographies. If you need help drafting these materials, use the CEU Information & Question Generator tab on this page.

How do I make changes to my session or speaker information?

You can make many updates by logging into the Speaker Portal & Questionnaire using the email address associated with your speaker profile. The portal allows you to review and update speaker profile information such as name, organization, title, biography, and related details. It may also allow updates to certain session details, depending on where we are in the planning process.

For changes that affect who is listed as a speaker, facilitator, panelist, or host, please contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

How do I log into Attendee Hub?

Once Attendee Hub is available for the 2026 Global Gathering, presenters will receive login instructions by email. Please use the same email address connected to your registration and speaker profile.

When will presenters gain access to Attendee Hub?

Attendee Hub will open before the event. Registered presenters will receive information by email with instructions for accessing the platform, viewing the schedule, finding their session page, and preparing to host or participate in their session(s). This page will also be updated with additional Attendee Hub guidance as it becomes available.

What does registration give presenters access to?

Registration gives presenters access to the virtual event platform, live sessions during the Global Gathering, available session materials, connection opportunities, and eligible on-demand recordings after the event. Presenters must use the same email address connected to their registration and speaker profile when accessing Attendee Hub.

I am not sure what email is associated with my registration. How do I find that out?

Look for your 2026 Global Gathering registration confirmation email. The email address that received your confirmation is likely the address connected to your registration. If you are still unsure, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

I cannot find my verification code to access Attendee Hub. What should I do?

First, confirm that you are using the same email address connected to your registration and speaker profile. Then check your spam or junk folder for the verification code. If you are still unable to access Attendee Hub, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

How do I start or host my conference session?

In Attendee Hub, search for your session from the schedule and open the session page. If you are assigned as a host, you should see the host access option when host access is available. Do not join only as a participant when you are preparing to host. Additional host-access instructions will be added here once Attendee Hub is finalized for 2026.

I cannot find my session in Attendee Hub. What should I do?

Try searching with a shorter keyword or phrase from your session title. For example, if your session has a longer title, search using two or three distinctive words rather than the full title. You may also be able to use schedule filters such as date, time, session type, or speaker. If you still cannot find your session, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

Will my session be recorded?

Many sessions will be recorded and made available after the event through the on-demand video library. Recording status may vary by session. Use the Session Information Lookup tab to review available recording information for your session. If the recording status does not look correct, contact the Global Gathering Team.

Why don't I see a "Join as host" button in Attendee Hub?

Host access may not appear until Attendee Hub and session permissions are fully configured for the 2026 Global Gathering. If host access is available and you still do not see the host option, the most likely reason is that your speaker profile has not yet been assigned as a host for that session. Please contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu so we can review your session setup.

Something about my session does not look correct. Can I make edits to the name or description of my session? Can I change who is listed as a speaker?

Please first check the Speaker Portal & Questionnaire to see whether the information can be updated directly. If the change affects the session title, description, listed speakers, facilitators, panelists, or host assignments, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu. The team uses speaker and host assignments to manage session access in Attendee Hub, so those changes need to be reviewed carefully.

Who should I contact if I need help?

If you have questions about your session, speaker profile, registration status, Attendee Hub access, CEU materials, or presenter requirements, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

CEU Information

Some sessions at the 2026 Global Gathering may be eligible for Continuing Education Units, or CEUs. CEU-eligible sessions require three knowledge-check questions and three measurable learning objectives. These materials help support the post-session CEU process for eligible participants.

Knowledge-check questions should be based on the content of the session itself. They should help confirm that participants engaged with key ideas, concepts, frameworks, or practices shared during the session.

Please submit three knowledge-check questions. True/False or Multiple Choice questions are preferred.

Please submit three measurable learning objectives that describe what participants should be able to understand, describe, identify, apply, or reflect on after the session.

Questions and objectives should be specific to your session content and should not be about the Global Gathering itself.

Please submit CEU materials through the Speaker Portal & Questionnaire by the deadline communicated by the Global Gathering Team. If the portal is closed or you need support, contact globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.
