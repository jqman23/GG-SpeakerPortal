Overview

Welcome to the 2026 Global Gathering Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page. This page brings together practical information to help presenters prepare for A Global Gathering for the Future of Child Welfare. We recommend bookmarking this page and completing the Speaker Survey using the tab above, where you can submit required speaker information directly to the Global Gathering Team.

As the event approaches, this page will continue to be updated with guidance about Attendee Hub, session hosting, CEU requirements, resource deadlines, and other presenter supports.

Required next step: complete the Speaker Survey

Please complete the Speaker Survey for your session as your first priority. Your responses confirm important session details, including CEU materials, recording status, video format, and any pre-recording plans that apply to your session.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I use polls during my session?

Yes. Polling options may be available depending on your session format in Attendee Hub. Additional 2026 guidance will be added here once the final speaker resource guide is available. If you have questions about how polls may work for your session, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

How do I know if my session is in an Embedded or regular Zoom video format?

Use the Session Information Lookup tab above and follow the prompts. If video format information is not yet listed, please check back closer to the Gathering or contact the Global Gathering Team.

How do I know if my session is CEU Eligible?

Use the Session Information Lookup tab above and follow the prompts. CEU eligibility will also be reflected in Attendee Hub once the program and session details are finalized.

What is the 2026 Global Gathering, and when does it take place?

A Global Gathering for the Future of Child Welfare is a three-day virtual convening taking place October 6–8, 2026. Day 1 features Skill Building Institutes, and Days 2 and 3 feature the Global Gathering program, including keynotes, workshops, solution-oriented strategy sessions, international exchanges, creative spaces, and opportunities for connection. This Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page is designed to help presenters prepare for their role in that virtual experience.

What are Skill Building Institutes?

Skill Building Institutes are immersive, hands-on learning experiences offered on Day 1 of the Gathering. These extended sessions are designed for practical skill development, deeper learning, and applied frameworks that support future-focused child welfare practice and leadership. If you are presenting or supporting a Skill Building Institute, please pay close attention to any additional instructions from the Global Gathering Team, since these sessions may require more preparation than shorter sessions.

What do CEU-eligible presenters need to submit?

For CEU-eligible sessions, at least one presenter should submit three knowledge-check questions and three measurable learning objectives through the Speaker Survey tab on this page by the deadline communicated by the Global Gathering Team. Questions should be based on the content of the session itself and should not be about the Global Gathering, the session format, or presenter biographies. A simple draft helper appears inside the Speaker Survey for CEU-eligible sessions.

How do I make changes to my session or speaker information?

For updates to your session or speaker information, please contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu. The team can update speaker profile details such as name, organization, title, and biography, as well as session details depending on where we are in the planning process.

For changes that affect who is listed as a speaker, facilitator, panelist, or host, please contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

How do I log into Attendee Hub?

Once Attendee Hub is available for the 2026 Global Gathering, presenters will receive login instructions by email. Please use the email address connected to your speaker profile.

When will presenters gain access to Attendee Hub?

Attendee Hub will open before the event. Presenters will receive information by email with instructions for accessing the platform, viewing the schedule, finding their session page, and preparing to host or participate in their session(s). This page will also be updated with additional Attendee Hub guidance as it becomes available.

I cannot find my verification code to access Attendee Hub. What should I do?

First, confirm that you are using the same email address connected to your speaker profile. Then check your spam or junk folder for the verification code. If you are still unable to access Attendee Hub, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

How do I start or host my conference session?

In Attendee Hub, search for your session from the schedule and open the session page. If you are assigned as a host, you should see the host access option when host access is available. Do not join only as a participant when you are preparing to host. Additional host-access instructions will be added here once Attendee Hub is finalized for 2026.

I cannot find my session in Attendee Hub. What should I do?

Try searching with a shorter keyword or phrase from your session title. For example, if your session has a longer title, search using two or three distinctive words rather than the full title. You may also be able to use schedule filters such as date, time, session type, or speaker. If you still cannot find your session, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

Will my session be recorded?

Many sessions will be recorded and made available after the event through the on-demand video library. Recording status may vary by session. Use the Session Information Lookup tab to review available recording information for your session. If the recording status does not look correct, contact the Global Gathering Team.

Why don't I see a "Join as host" button in Attendee Hub?

Host access may not appear until Attendee Hub and session permissions are fully configured for the 2026 Global Gathering. If host access is available and you still do not see the host option, the most likely reason is that your speaker profile has not yet been assigned as a host for that session. Please contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu so we can review your session setup.

Something about my session does not look correct. Can I make edits to the name or description of my session? Can I change who is listed as a speaker?

Please contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu to request a correction. If the change affects the session title, description, listed speakers, facilitators, panelists, or host assignments, the team will need to review and update those details carefully as they affect session access in Attendee Hub. The team uses speaker and host assignments to manage session access in Attendee Hub, so those changes need to be reviewed carefully.

Who should I contact if I need help?

If you have questions about your session, speaker profile, Attendee Hub access, CEU materials, or presenter requirements, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.
