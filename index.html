Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

|
Do presenters need to register for the event?

Yes. Presenters are expected to register for Days 2 and 3 of the 2026 Global Gathering if they have not already done so. Registration ensures presenters can access the virtual event space and take part in the full learning experience.

How is the 2026 Global Gathering structured?

A Global Gathering for the Future of Child Welfare is a three-day virtual convening taking place October 6–8, 2026 (MDT).

Day 1 features Skill Building Institutes, which are longer, hands-on learning experiences. Days 2 and 3 include keynotes, workshops, strategy sessions, international exchanges, and creative spaces.

As a speaker, what do I need to do to prepare for the event?

Please complete the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. This is the most important next step because it helps the Global Gathering Team confirm your session details, CEU needs, recording status, video format, and any pre-recording plans.

You should also review your session information and monitor your email for additional instructions on how to access Attendee Hub, the online event center, as the event gets closer. If your session involves multiple presenters, please coordinate among your co-presenters to ensure one person completes the Questionnaire for your session.

If you have not already registered, please also register for the 2026 Global Gathering before the early-bird deadline to receive the best registration rate.

What is the Speaker Questionnaire?

The Speaker Questionnaire is the required form presenters should complete for each session they are presenting. The form asks you to confirm key details about your presentation.

The Questionnaire is session-specific, so the questions you see may change depending on the session you select. Only one presenter from each session needs to complete this form.

Do I need to complete the Speaker Questionnaire if I already updated my information via the Speaker Portal?

Yes. The Speaker Portal and Speaker Questionnaire serve different purposes.

Even if you updated your speaker or session profile in the Speaker Portal, you still need to complete the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. This Questionnaire helps the Global Gathering Team confirm session details and ensure a smooth virtual experience for all.

How do I complete the Speaker Questionnaire?

Open the Speaker Questionnaire tab, select your session, answer the questions that appear, and submit.

If you are presenting more than one session, please complete the Questionnaire once for each session. Only one speaker needs to complete the Questionnaire for each session.

How do I check my session information?

Use the Session Information Lookup tab to search by speaker name or session title. This will show the session information currently on file, including recording status, CEU eligibility, and video format.

If something does not look right or you have any questions about your session, please contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

My session type or duration looks different from what I originally submitted. Is that correct?

Likely, yes. As we finalized the 2026 Global Gathering program, some sessions were adjusted in session type or duration to better fit the overall schedule and programming structure.

The session details shown on this page — including in the Session Information Lookup and the Speaker Questionnaire — reflect the most current information we have on file for your session.

If you have questions about your session type or duration, or would like more context about any changes, please reach out to us at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu. We're happy to walk through the details with you.

If my session is CEU-eligible, what do I need to submit?

For CEU-eligible sessions, please submit 3 measurable learning objectives and 3 knowledge-check questions through the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. You can auto-generate these objectives and questions through the Questionnaire. We recommend reviewing any auto-generated information before submitting the Questionnaire.

Will my session be recorded?

To see if your session is currently set to be recorded, please complete the Speaker Questionnaire or use the Session Information Lookup tab. If you choose to record your session, you will have access to it after the event concludes.

What is the difference between a Zoom and Embedded session?

Zoom sessions use a standard Zoom-based setup connected to the virtual event experience. Embedded sessions are built more directly into Attendee Hub, the online event platform. You can view the differences by clicking here.

What are Skill Building Institutes?

Skill Building Institutes are extended, hands-on learning sessions offered on Day 1 of the Global Gathering. They are designed for deeper practice, applied tools, and skill development.

Skill Building Institute presenters will receive additional information from the Global Gathering Team as the event nears.

What is Attendee Hub, and when will I receive access to this platform?

Attendee Hub is the online event center for the Global Gathering. Presenters will receive Attendee Hub access at the beginning of September by email. Attendee Hub is where presenters will log in to begin, host, and access their virtual session(s).

Additional Attendee Hub resources will be added to this page as the event nears. Presenters will also be notified by email.

How do I host or start my session?

Once Attendee Hub has launched, the Global Gathering Team will share instructions by email to presenters for accessing and starting their sessions.

Can my organization register as a group?

Yes. Group registration is available for organizations purchasing 10 or more registrations and offers reduced per-person rates. Many agencies, universities, nonprofits, training programs, and community partners use group registration to bring whole teams into the Global Gathering as a shared learning experience.

2026 group rates:

  • 10–19 registrants: $150/person
  • 20–29 registrants: $135/person
  • 30–39 registrants: $120/person
  • 40–49 registrants: $105/person
  • 50+ registrants: $90/person

For additional information, visit the Group Registrations page.

Who do I contact for support?

Email the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu. Please include your name, session title, and a short description of what you need support with so we can navigate you to the right resource.
