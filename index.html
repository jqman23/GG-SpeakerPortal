What is the 2026 Global Gathering?

A Global Gathering for the Future of Child Welfare is a three-day virtual convening taking place October 6–8, 2026. The event brings together people from around the world to imagine, discuss, and build toward the future of child, youth, family, and community well-being.

Day 1 features Skill Building Institutes, which are longer, hands-on learning experiences. Days 2 and 3 include keynotes, workshops, strategy sessions, international exchanges, and creative spaces.

As a speaker, what do I need to do before the event?

Please complete the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. This is the most important next step because it helps the Global Gathering Team confirm your session details, CEU needs, recording status, video format, and any pre-recording plans.

If you have not already registered, please also register for the 2026 Global Gathering before the early-bird deadline to receive the best registration rate.

What is this Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page?

This page is your preparation hub for the 2026 Global Gathering. It includes the Speaker Questionnaire, session lookup tool, FAQs, Attendee Hub updates, and presenter support information.

Use this page to complete the Speaker Questionnaire and access resources.

What is the Speaker Questionnaire?

The Speaker Questionnaire is the required form presenters complete for each session they are presenting. It asks you to confirm key details such as CEU materials, recording status, video format, pre-recording plans, and any questions or access needs.

The Questionnaire is session-specific, so the questions you see may change depending on the session you select. Only one presenter from each session needs to complete this form.

What is the difference between the Speaker Portal and this Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page?

The Speaker Portal and the Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page serve different purposes.

Speaker Portal

Your go-to place for:

  • Updating your session information
  • Uploading session materials
  • Updating your speaker profile
  • Uploading a speaker image
  • Staying organized and checking off tasks

Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page

Your go-to place for:

  • Completing the Speaker Questionnaire
  • Looking up session details such as CEU eligibility, recording status, and video format
  • Preparing for Attendee Hub access as the event nears
  • Accessing FAQs and presenter resources

The Speaker Portal is used to review or update speaker and session profile information, such as speaker details, bios, and session descriptions.

This Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page populates the required Speaker Questionnaire, along with FAQs, Attendee Hub guidance, and other support resources.

Do I need to complete the Speaker Questionnaire if I already updated my information via the Speaker Portal?

Yes. The Speaker Portal and Speaker Questionnaire serve different purposes.

Even if you updated your speaker or session profile in the Speaker Portal, you still need to complete the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. The Questionnaire helps the Global Gathering Team confirm session logistics that are not directly handled through the Speaker Portal, including CEU related information, recording status, video format, and pre-recording plans.

How do I complete the Speaker Questionnaire?

Open the Speaker Questionnaire tab, select your session, answer the questions that appear, and submit.

If you are presenting more than one session, please complete the Questionnaire once for each session. Only one speaker needs to complete the Questionnaire for each session.

How do I check my session information?

Use the Session Information Lookup tab to search by speaker name or session title. This will show the session information currently on file, including recording status, CEU eligibility, and video format.

If something does not look right, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

If my session is CEU-eligible, what do I need to submit?

For CEU-eligible sessions, please submit 3 measurable learning objectives and 3 knowledge-check questions through the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. You can auto-generate these objectives and questions through the Questionnaire. We recommend reviewing any auto-generated information before submitting the Questionnaire.

Will my session be recorded?

To see if your session is currently set to be recorded, please complete the Speaker Questionnaire or use the Session Information Lookup tab. If you choose to record your session, you will have access to it after the event concludes.

What is the difference between a Zoom and Embedded session?

Zoom sessions use a standard Zoom-based setup connected to the virtual event experience. Embedded sessions are built more directly into Attendee Hub, the online event platform. You can view the differences by clicking here.

What are Skill Building Institutes?

Skill Building Institutes are extended, hands-on learning sessions offered on Day 1 of the Global Gathering. They are designed for deeper practice, applied tools, and skill development.

Skill Building Institute presenters will receive additional information from the Global Gathering Team as the event nears.

Do presenters need to register for the event?

Yes. Presenters are expected to register for the 2026 Global Gathering if they have not already done so. Registration is needed so presenters can access the virtual event experience. We hope presenters will choose to participate in other aspects of the Global Gathering.

Please register before the early-bird deadline (July 31, 2026) in order to secure the best registration rate.

When will I receive Attendee Hub access?

Presenters will receive Attendee Hub access at the beginning of September by email.

Additional Attendee Hub resources and presenter guidance will be added to this page as the event setup is finalized.

How do I host or start my session?

Once Attendee Hub is accessible, the Global Gathering Team will share instructions for accessing and starting your session.

Who do I contact for support?

Email the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu. Please include your name, session title, and a short description of what you need so we can help more quickly.
