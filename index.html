Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the 2026 Global Gathering?

A Global Gathering for the Future of Child Welfare is a three-day virtual convening taking place October 6–8, 2026. The event brings together people from around the world to imagine, discuss, and build toward the future of child, youth, family, and community well-being.

Day 1 features Skill Building Institutes, which are longer, hands-on learning experiences. Days 2 and 3 include keynotes, workshops, strategy sessions, international exchanges, creative spaces, and opportunities for connection.

What is the Speaker Questionnaire?

The Speaker Questionnaire is the required form presenters complete for each session they are presenting. It asks you to confirm key details such as CEU materials, recording status, video format, pre-recording plans, and any questions or access needs.

The questionnaire is session-specific, so the questions you see may change depending on the session you select.

What is the difference between the Speaker Portal and this Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page?

The Speaker Portal and the Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page serve different purposes.

The Speaker Portal is used to review or update speaker and session profile information, such as speaker details, bios, session descriptions, and related program information.

This Speaker Resource & Questionnaire Page is focused on presenter preparation. It includes the required Speaker Questionnaire, session lookup, FAQs, Attendee Hub guidance, CEU information, and other support resources.

If you need to confirm logistics, complete the Speaker Questionnaire. If you need to update speaker or session profile information, use the Speaker Portal or contact the Global Gathering Team.

Do I need to complete the Speaker Questionnaire if I already used the Speaker Portal?

Yes. The Speaker Portal and Speaker Questionnaire collect different information.

Even if your speaker or session profile is already updated in the Speaker Portal, please complete the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026. The questionnaire helps us confirm session logistics that may not be handled through the Speaker Portal, including CEU materials, recording status, video format, and pre-recording plans.

How do I complete the Speaker Questionnaire?

Open the Speaker Questionnaire tab, search for your session title, select the correct session, and answer the questions that appear.

If you are presenting more than one session, please complete the questionnaire once for each session.

Some questions only appear when they apply to your session. For example, CEU questions only appear for CEU-eligible sessions, and pre-recording questions only appear when pre-recording interest is listed for that session.

How do I check my session details?

Use the Session Information Lookup tab to search by speaker name or session title. This will show the session information currently on file, including recording status, CEU eligibility, and video format.

If something does not look right, contact the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu.

What do CEU-eligible presenters need to submit?

For CEU-eligible sessions, please submit 3 measurable learning objectives and 3 knowledge-check questions through the Speaker Questionnaire by August 7, 2026.

The objectives should describe what participants will be able to do, understand, or reflect on after the session. The knowledge-check questions should help confirm participant learning and may be single-answer multiple choice or True/False.

The Speaker Questionnaire includes a built-in draft helper that can auto-generate a starting point for your CEU objectives and questions using your session title and description. Please review and edit anything generated before submitting.

Will my session be recorded?

Recording status varies by session. Select your session in the Speaker Questionnaire or use the Session Information Lookup tab to review what we currently have on file.

If the recording status shown does not look correct, you can flag that in the Speaker Questionnaire or contact the Global Gathering Team.

What is the difference between Zoom and Embedded?

Zoom sessions use a standard Zoom-based setup connected to the virtual event experience. Embedded sessions are built more directly into Attendee Hub and may offer some features differently.

The Speaker Questionnaire includes a Zoom vs Embedded comparison pop-up so you can review the differences before confirming the format currently listed for your session.

If your session depends on a specific feature, such as breakout rooms, Q&A, polls, screen sharing, participant video/audio, captions, or transcripts, please review the Zoom vs Embedded comparison and let us know in the Speaker Questionnaire.

What are Skill Building Institutes?

Skill Building Institutes are extended, hands-on learning sessions offered on Day 1 of the Global Gathering. They are designed for deeper practice, applied tools, and skill development.

Because these sessions are longer and more interactive, presenters may receive additional preparation guidance from the Global Gathering Team.

Do presenters need to register for the event?

Yes. Presenters should register for the 2026 Global Gathering if they have not already done so. Registration is needed so presenters can access the virtual event experience and participate in the Gathering.

Please register before the early-bird deadline.

When will I get Attendee Hub access?

Presenters will receive Attendee Hub instructions by email before the event. Please use the email address connected to your speaker profile when accessing the platform.

Additional Attendee Hub resources and presenter guidance will be added to this page as the event setup is finalized.

How do I host or start my session?

Once Attendee Hub is ready, the Global Gathering Team will share instructions for accessing and starting your session. If you are assigned as a host, you will access your session through the session page in Attendee Hub.

More detailed host-access guidance will be shared closer to the event.

Who do I contact for help?

Email the Global Gathering Team at globalgathering@cuanschutz.edu. Please include your name, session title, and a short description of what you need so we can help more quickly.
